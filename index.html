About our Company

Since 2017, Triple Zee Trading and Services has been a trusted provider of all types of cleaning chemicals and supplies for businesses, households, and institutions in Qatar. From disinfectants to eco-friendly options, we ensure the highest quality in every product.

In addition to our comprehensive range of cleaning materials, we offer expert cleaning and manpower services. Whether you need routine cleaning or specialized manpower solutions, our dedicated team delivers efficient, reliable service, ensuring your spaces remain spotless and well-maintained.
